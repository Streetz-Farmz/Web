About us

We didn’t start a brand. We got tired of the noise.

The market is full of product with no paper trail and names that mean nothing. Streetz Farmz exists because someone had to take it seriously.

We go direct. We verify. We document every batch from the mountain to the club. Not because it’s trendy — because it’s the only way to know what you’re actually handling.

Rif heritage is centuries deep. The least we can do is respect it.

Streetz Heritage

The Rif didn’t wait to be discovered. Its soil, altitude, and generational knowledge were already producing some of the world’s most respected resin long before anyone put a label on it. Streetz Farmz carries that lineage into global culture — same roots, sharper eye, no apologies.
